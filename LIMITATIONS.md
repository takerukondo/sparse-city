# Limitations / disclaimers — sparse-city

**This is experimental software.** Not a planning tool for real cities, agencies, or communities.

## Non-goals

1. Not a maze game without abstain metrics
2. Not a Planet City (or any) exhibition remount / visual brand grid
3. Not POPGym clone completeness
4. Not real LIDAR, Zillow, Yelp, Twitter, or commercial listing layers
5. Not risk scoring, credit, policing, or targeting of real people
6. Not a representation of Somaliland, Hargeisa, Los Angeles, or any real community's knowledge, consent, or priorities
7. Not SCI-Arc official research output
8. No GitHub org publish / deploy from this worktree by default

## Limitations

- Tiny 3×3 synthetic grid; hand-written price table
- Baselines are scripted heuristics, not trained policies
- “Land use” / “hazard” tokens are invented labels for eval, not GIS layers
- Scorecard is a toy metric (λ, μ, δ), not a validated decision-quality measure
- Academic prior art (POPGym, abstention papers) is cited as related work, not embedded code
- `takeru_role: unknown` on source projects — do not claim sole authorship of SCI-Arc research

## Ethics reminder

Mechanisms about sparse data and abstention are **portable evaluation ideas**. Do not reverse-map synthetic Toyville outcomes onto real places or speak *for* communities named in lineage source cards.
