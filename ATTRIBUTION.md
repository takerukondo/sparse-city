# Research lineage and credits

This is a personal software project by Takeru Kondo, not an official SCI-Arc publication. Takeru worked as a research assistant at SCI-Arc Research from May 2024 to January 2025; this repository does not claim that he was the sole author of any source project below.

- **Views of Planet City** — Liam Young, Casey Rehm, John Cooper, Jennifer Chen, Damjan Jovanovic, Angelica Lorenzi, Namik Mackic. [Project page](http://research.sciarc.edu/projects/11-planet-city-exhibition-2020-)
- **How Cities See** — Benjamin Bratton, Casey Rehm, Laure Michelon. [Project page](http://research.sciarc.edu/projects/06-how-cities-see-course-2020-)
- **Backyard Home Data Explorer** — Mimi Zeiger, Casey Rehm, Yundi Zhang, Yashwanth Munuhoti, Saghyun Suh, and the additional contributors listed on the project page. [Project page](http://research.sciarc.edu/projects/15-backyard-home-data-explorer)
- **Multifamily Housing in Somaliland** — Frederik Emil Seehusen, Masha Hupalo, Anders Michelsen, Rashid Ali, Artem Panchenko, Emily Dinnerman, Nicholas Gochnour, Esin Karaosman, Malvin Wibowo, Lance Arevalo; with the partner institutions listed on the project page. [Project page](http://research.sciarc.edu/projects/08-multifamily-housing-in-somaliland-research-2020-)

Only general research questions were carried into this implementation. All runtime data is synthetic.
